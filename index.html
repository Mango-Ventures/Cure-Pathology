Our Services

Tests we perform

Comprehensive panels for preventive and diagnostic needs — accurate results, fast turnaround.

🩸
Complete Blood Count (CBC)
Checks RBCs, WBCs, hemoglobin, and platelets. Essential for routine health monitoring.
🫀
Lipid Profile
Cholesterol, triglycerides, LDL/HDL — critical for heart health assessment.
🦋
Thyroid Function (T3/T4/TSH)
Detects hypo/hyperthyroidism. Recommended for fatigue, weight changes, and mood disorders.
🍬
Blood Sugar / HbA1c
Fasting, PP, and 3-month average glucose levels for diabetes management.
🫁
Liver Function Test (LFT)
SGPT, SGOT, bilirubin — monitors liver health and medication effects.
🔬
Kidney Function Test (KFT)
Creatinine, urea, eGFR — essential for kidney disease detection and monitoring.
🧬
Urine Routine & Microscopy
Detects infections, kidney disorders, and diabetes markers through urine analysis.
💉
Full Body Checkup Package
Comprehensive 40+ parameter panel covering heart, liver, kidney, diabetes, thyroid.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about tests, reports, home collection and booking at Cure Pathology.

Does Cure Pathology offer home sample collection in Muzaffarpur?
Yes. Cure Pathology offers free home sample collection across Muzaffarpur. Book on WhatsApp or through the online form, and a trained phlebotomist will visit your home to collect the sample — no travel needed.
How soon do I get my test reports?
Most routine tests such as CBC, blood sugar and thyroid are ready the same day. Reports are shared on WhatsApp and can also be downloaded online.
What are the timings of Cure Pathology?
Cure Pathology is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday & Sunday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
How can I book a blood test at Cure Pathology?
You can book online using the “Book your test online” form on this page, call 091020 42222, or message the lab on WhatsApp. Choose your test, share your details, and the team confirms your slot.
